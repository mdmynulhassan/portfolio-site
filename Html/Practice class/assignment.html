Samvat Rice

Navratri is here, and if you are looking to gorge on some delicious Navratri food, then Samvat Rice recipe is perfect for you! Prepared using barnyard millet or samak rice (otherwise known as samvat rice), potatoes, turmeric, black pepper, sendha namak, refined oil and green chillies; this is a delectable main dish recipe that can be enjoyed for lunch as well as dinner.

You can pair this North Indian recipe with beaten curd to make it into a flavorful meal that not only will satiates your hunger but also satisfy your foodie soul! Ideally prepared during Navratri or vrat, this main dish recipe can also be made on occasions like kitty parties and potlucks. So, prepare this easy recipe at home and indulge in its mouth-watering taste!


Chicken Kosha

Chicken Kosha is an aunthentic Bengali recipe that is a favorite among the chicken lovers all over the world. Made using chicken, potatoes, tomatoes, green chillies, ginger, garlic, bay leaf, cloves, Kashmiri red chilli, green cardamom, cinnamon and salt, this main dish recipe is perfect to be enjoyed for lunch as well as dinner. You can also serve this non-vegetarian recipe with rice or chapati to make it even more flavorful.

The best thing about this savory delight is that it is made with wholesome spices that are slow-cooked on low heat to make it absolutely mouth-watering. Occasions like kitty parties, potlucks, buffets and game nights are apt to relish this simple recipe and will surely astound everyone with its zesty flavors. So, try this easy recipe this weekend and indulge in its lip-smacking taste!


Italian Beef Steak

Italian Beef Steak is a scrumptious Italian recipe that will be loved by all the beef lovers. This easy-to-make recipe will be a great lunch or dinner for you and your family. Loaded with proteins and vitamins, this dish will be apt to provide your body with the essential nutrients.

Made with the goodness of beef, worcestershire sauce, bay leaves, peppercorns, ghee and black pepper, this flavorful recipe will be a treat for your taste buds. Pair this recipe with a tall glass of red wine and dwell into the world of tempting flavors. Enrich your taste palates with this moth-watering recipe and serve it to your loved ones on special occasions to relish its lip-smacking flavors with them.


Fish Makhani

Fish Makhani is a mouth-watering North Indian recipe that is quite popular among the seafood lovers. This main dish recipe is a delicious amalgamation of fish fillets with a buttery gravy and can be enjoyed for lunch as well as dinner. You can serve this non-vegetarian recipe with steamed rice or chapati to make it into a complete meal.

The taste of perfectly fried fish fillets, coated in creamy tomato gravy tantalizes your taste buds and makes you crave for more. Occasions like kitty parties, potlucks and buffets are apt to relish this lip-smacking delicacy and will surely impress your guests with its rich and luscious flavours. So, don't hold on any longer and prepare this easy recipe for your family this weekend!
